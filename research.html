Major Publications

Synergistic adverse effects of microfibers and freshwater acidification on host-microbiota interactions in the water flea Daphnia magna

Author: Young Hwan Lee, et al. (2024)
Journal: Marine Ecology Progress Series
This study explores how marine species adapt genetically to ocean warming and acidification using transcriptome and epigenome analysis.

DNA metabarcoding for biodiversity assessment in coastal lagoons

Author: Y. Lee, B. Shim (2022)
Journal: Frontiers in Marine Science
A comprehensive study applying eDNA metabarcoding techniques to monitor biodiversity shifts in semi-enclosed lagoon systems.
